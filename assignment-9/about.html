Ballet: How do you start? What skills should you have?

How would you know where to start?

In almost every city there is a ballet studio or ballet classes being offered at a dance studio. When thinking of how to start asses your age, experience, and talent. Usually classes are structured by age! Reach out to a studio near you for more information. If you are a beginner no skills are need however being willing to learn and have a long attention span will make you more successful.

What do you wear to ballet?

  • Pink Tights
  • Leotard (colors will vary)
  • Ballet slippers
  • Pointe Shoes
  • Any form of warmups

What to pack in your dance bag?

What to pack in your dance bag is an essentail question. You will want to bring an extra set of slippers, tights, and leotard if possible. Having hair ties, bobby pins, and a brush can help you accomplish a great bun. Extra padding and lambswool will be helpful once pointshoes are part of your ballet class. A snack and water is also nice!
