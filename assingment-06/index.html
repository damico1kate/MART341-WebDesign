No Bake Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Mild
  • 1 stick of Butter
  • 1/4 cup Unsweetend Cocoa Powder
  • 3 cups Oats
  • 1 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1 tbs Vanilla Extract
  • pinch Kosher Salt

Directions

  1. Line a baking sheet with wax paper (parchment works too)
  2. In a mideaum saucepan over medium heat let the sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa mix and boil for 1 minute.
  3. Remove from heat and add oats, peanut butter, vanilla, and salt, and stir to mix.
  4. Drop small teaspoons of mixture onto the backing sheet and let the cookies sit at room temperature (72 o degrees fahrenheit)
  5. Refridgerate for 30 minutes, then enjoy!
