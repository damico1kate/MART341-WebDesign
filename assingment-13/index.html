The place I call home is...

Sequim Washington

The city of sunshine and well known lavendar!




Located on the Olympic Peninsula Sequim is a small town in Washington. Known for being the "Lavendar Capital of North America" as well as the delicious Dungeness crab. Also known as the sunshine city due to being one of the driest cities in the state. Surrounded by ocean and mountains Sequim is a great place to live or visit!

— Official City of Sequim Website.































The town Sequim as of 2021 had a population of 8,241 people. Resident numbers have been increasing over the years due to travel exposure.
