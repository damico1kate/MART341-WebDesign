The Local Events!

Interested in visiting Sequim?
Here are some events to look into!

Lavendar Festival

Every year mid July the city of Sequim puts on a Lavendar Festival. This festival includes selling of every imaginable lavendar product. Live music and vendors come to different farms to create the summer life event. As said on the environment page Sequim is the lavendar capital.There are many farms but in Sequim and in the U.S.A the largest farm is the Sequim-Dungeness Vally lavendar farm!

Irrigation Parade

Along with the annual lavendar festival there is the Irrigation Festival. This festival was made becuase of the hard work of the settlers who made irrigation ditches so that Sequim has water. Every year the town comes together and has a parade which includes all of the community. This is held early May and is an annual event!

Unity of Effort

The "Unity of Effort" is the celebration and recognition of all types of first responders. This is not held by teh city but by a company called Security Services. All types of people from the community come for free and is open to the public. There are many booths where first responders will host and people can learn more about what they do. Every year there is the army, police, firemen, and more!
